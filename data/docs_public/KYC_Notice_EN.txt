1. Why We Ask for Your Documents
FinSight Bank must identify all clients before opening or maintaining an account under the Know Your Customer (KYC) framework.
This helps protect you and the financial system from fraud, money laundering, and terrorism financing.
Verification ensures secure and compliant transactions in line with Tunisian and international regulations.

2. What We Verify
FinSight verifies your identity, residence, source of income or funds, and beneficial ownership if representing another person or entity.

3. Required Documents
Type of Client | Mandatory Documents
Individual (Resident) | National ID or Passport + Proof of Residence + Proof of Income
Non‑Resident | Passport + Residence Permit + Proof of Address Abroad + Source of Funds
Company / Legal Entity | Certificate of Incorporation + Statutes + Tax ID + Shareholders List + Manager ID
Association or NGO | Legal Recognition Decree + Board Resolution + Representative ID
Additional documents may be required for foreign transfers or high‑value transactions.

4. How We Protect Your Information
Your personal data are confidential and processed in compliance with Law No. 2004‑63, the Bank’s Data Protection Policy, and BCT regulations.
Information is never shared without legal authorization. Access is restricted to authorized staff and stored securely.

5. How Often We Update Your Information
FinSight may request updated documents when your ID or address changes, when opening new products, or when transactions are large or unusual.
Keeping your data current avoids service interruptions or delays.

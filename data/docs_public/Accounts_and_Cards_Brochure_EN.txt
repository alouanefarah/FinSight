4. Lost or Stolen Card Procedure
1. Immediate Blocking – call 71 000 123 or use FinSight Mobile “Block Card” option.
2. Declaration – report to the police within 24h.
3. Replacement – bring declaration to branch; new card within 5 working days.
4. Fee – free if theft (with report), otherwise 15 TND.

1. Purpose
This procedure defines how FinSight Bank handles customer complaints in accordance with the Central Bank of Tunisia’s directives on client protection.
Its purpose is to ensure fair, transparent, and timely responses to any complaint related to banking products or services.

2. Principles
FinSight Bank’s complaint management is based on accessibility, transparency, impartiality, and traceability.

3. Channels for Submitting Complaints
1. In Branch: submit a written complaint to your advisor.
2. By Email: complaints@finsightbank.tn with full name, ID, and contact.
3. By Mail: FinSight Bank – Customer Relations Department, 12 Rue du Lac, Les Berges du Lac, Tunis 1053.
4. Online Form: www.finsightbank.tn/complaints
5. Mobile App: under “Support” → “Submit Complaint”.

4. Acknowledgement and Processing
Clients receive an acknowledgement within 48 hours. The Bank resolves complaints within 15 working days. If further analysis is needed, the client is informed of the new deadline.

5. Investigation and Resolution
Complaints are analyzed by the Customer Relations Department with the relevant branch. Clients receive a written response summarizing the outcome. If unsatisfied, clients may request a review by the Compliance Department.

6. Escalation and External Mediation
If unresolved after 30 days, clients may contact the Central Bank of Tunisia – Customer Protection Department or the Financial Sector Mediator, providing all prior correspondence.

7. Confidentiality and Data Protection
All complaints are confidential. Data collected are used only for complaint management and processed in compliance with the Information Security and Data Protection Policy and Tunisian Law No. 2004‑63.

8. Monitoring and Continuous Improvement
Complaint statistics are reviewed quarterly by the Risk and Compliance Committee and audited annually by Internal Audit.

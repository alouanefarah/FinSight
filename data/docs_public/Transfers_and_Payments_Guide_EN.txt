1. Overview
This guide explains how to perform transfers and payments securely via FinSight Online, FinSight Mobile, or branches. Transactions are designed to be simple, fast, and transparent.

2. Domestic Transfers
A domestic transfer is a money transfer within Tunisia.
Type | Description | Execution Time | Fee
Internal Transfer | Between FinSight accounts | Instant | Free
Interbank Transfer | To another Tunisian bank | 1 business day | 2 TND
Standing Order | Recurring payments | As scheduled | Free (setup via Online/Mobile)
Steps: Log in, select “Transfers”, enter beneficiary details, confirm with OTP.

3. International Transfers (SWIFT / IBAN)
International transfers allow sending or receiving funds abroad through SWIFT.
Required: beneficiary name, SWIFT/BIC code, IBAN, payment purpose.
Fees: Standard 0.15% (min 30 TND, max 300 TND); Express 0.25% (min 50 TND, max 400 TND).
Processing Time: 1–3 working days depending on destination.
Currencies: EUR, USD, GBP, and others authorized by the Central Bank of Tunisia.

4. Bill Payments
Clients can pay utility and service bills online.
Payable: electricity, water, telecom, taxes, tuition.
Channels | Availability | Confirmation
Online | 24/7 | Instant
Mobile | 24/7 | Instant
Branch | Business hours | Printed receipt

5. Mobile Transfers and QR Payments
Operation | Limit | Fee | Validation
Mobile Transfer (P2P) | 500 TND/day | Free | OTP
QR Merchant Payment | 2,000 TND/day | 0.5% | Instant confirmation

6. Payment Security
FinSight applies encryption, OTP authentication, timeouts, and fraud detection.
Tips: verify beneficiary IBAN, never share OTP, use secure devices and networks.

7. Troubleshooting and Assistance
Q: What if my transfer is delayed?
A: Check transfer history; contact support if pending after 48h.
Q: Can I cancel a transfer?
A: Only before execution; once processed, it cannot be reversed.
Q: How do I receive money from abroad?
A: Provide your IBAN and FinSight SWIFT code: FNSHTNTTXXX.

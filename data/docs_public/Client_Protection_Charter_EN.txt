1. Our Commitment
FinSight Bank ensures transparent, fair, and secure banking services. This Charter defines the principles protecting customer rights in line with Central Bank of Tunisia (BCT) and INPDP standards.

2. Transparency and Information
Clients receive clear, complete, and accessible information on all products and services. Contracts, fees, and conditions are provided in writing, with a 30-day notice before any modification.

3. Fairness and Non-Discrimination
FinSight treats all clients equally. Credit and service decisions are based on objective, financial criteria, without discrimination.

4. Data Confidentiality and Security
Personal data are protected under Law No. 2004‑63 and the Information Security Policy. Clients may access, correct, or request deletion of their data, and withdraw consent for optional processing.

5. Responsible Advice and Sales
Employees must provide honest and suitable advice. Recommendations are based on each client’s financial capacity. Compliance monitors sales practices to prevent abusive behavior.

6. Complaint Resolution and Mediation
Clients can file complaints free of charge. FinSight acknowledges within 48 hours and responds within 15 working days. Unresolved cases can be referred to the Financial Mediator or the Central Bank of Tunisia.

7. Digital Security and Awareness
FinSight applies encryption, authentication, and fraud monitoring. Clients receive awareness tips to protect against cyber risks and scams.

8. Financial Inclusion and Accessibility
The Bank promotes access to essential services through simplified accounts and support for students, retirees, and micro-entrepreneurs.

9. Client Responsibilities
Clients must provide accurate data, secure credentials, read terms before signing, and report suspicious activity promptly.

10. Continuous Improvement
The Charter is reviewed annually by the Risk and Compliance Committee to reflect new regulations and technologies, reaffirming FinSight’s commitment to responsible banking.

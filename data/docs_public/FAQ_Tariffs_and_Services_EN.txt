1. General Questions
Q1: Why does the bank charge account maintenance fees?
They cover the cost of managing your account, providing secure digital access, and processing transactions.
Q2: Are there accounts without maintenance fees?
Yes. The Youth Account (18–25) and Savings Accounts are free of monthly maintenance fees.
Q3: How will I know if fees change?
FinSight publishes all tariff updates 30 days before they take effect, online and in branches.

2. Cards and Withdrawals
Q4: What are the annual fees for FinSight cards?
Visa Classic 45 TND, Visa Gold 90 TND, Visa Platinum 150 TND, Visa Business 120 TND.
Q5: Are withdrawals free at all ATMs?
Free at FinSight ATMs; interbank withdrawals cost 1.5 TND.
Q6: What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
Block via FinSight Mobile “Block Card” or call 71 000 123. Replacement free if theft with police report.

3. Transfers and Payments
Q7: How much does a domestic transfer cost?
Standard transfer 2 TND; inter-branch within FinSight free.
Q8: What are international transfer fees?
0.15% of the amount (min 30 TND, max 300 TND).
Q9: How long does it take to receive an international transfer?
1–3 working days depending on destination.
Q10: Can I pay my bills online?
Yes, through FinSight Online or FinSight Mobile for utilities, telecom, and taxes.

4. Overdrafts and Loans
Q11: What is an overdraft facility?
A short-term advance allowing a negative balance up to an authorized limit; interest applies only to usage.
Q12: How is loan interest calculated?
Based on Money Market Average Rate (TMM) plus a contractual margin.
Q13: Can I repay my loan early?
Yes, at any time. Fixed-rate loans may include a small compensation fee.

5. Digital Banking and Security
Q14: Is FinSight Online secure?
Yes. It uses encryption, one-time passwords (OTP), and session timeouts.
Q15: Can I change daily transfer limits?
Yes, via FinSight Mobile → Settings → Transfer Limits.
Q16: What if I notice a suspicious transaction?
Contact Customer Service at 71 000 123 for investigation and refund if unauthorized.

6. Support and Contact
Q17: How can I contact the bank for more information?
Call +216 71 000 123, email support@finsightbank.tn, or visit www.finsightbank.tn.
Q18: Where can I find the full tariff brochure?
Available under “Transparence tarifaire” on the FinSight website.

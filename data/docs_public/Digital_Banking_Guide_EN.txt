1. Overview
FinSight Online and FinSight Mobile give you secure access to your accounts 24/7 — anywhere, anytime.
Manage your finances, pay bills, transfer funds, and control your cards instantly from your computer or smartphone.

2. Access and Login
FinSight Online (Web Portal):
1. Visit www.finsightbank.tn and click “Client Login”.
2. Enter your Client ID and password provided by the Bank.
3. Validate the login with the One-Time Code (OTP) sent to your registered phone.

FinSight Mobile (App):
1. Download “FinSight Mobile” from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Log in using the same credentials as FinSight Online.
3. Activate biometric login for future access (optional).

Security Tip: Never share your login details. FinSight will never ask for your PIN or password by phone or email.

3. Main Features
Function | Description
Account Overview | Real-time balance and transaction history.
Transfers | Domestic, inter-branch, and international (SWIFT/IBAN).
Payments | Pay utility bills, taxes, or telecom services instantly.
Standing Orders | Schedule automatic recurring transfers.
Card Management | Block/unblock cards, adjust spending limits, change PIN.
Notifications | Receive instant alerts for transactions or card activity.
Statement Download | Generate official statements in PDF.
FX Operations | Simulate and execute currency exchanges.

4. Security and Data Protection
FinSight uses encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud detection.
Sessions disconnect after 5 minutes of inactivity.
All data are hosted on secure servers compliant with INPDP and GDPR.

Best Practices:
• Keep your phone software up to date.
• Avoid public Wi-Fi for financial operations.
• Immediately block your account in case of suspicious activity.

6. Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What if I forget my password?
A: Click “Forgot Password” on the login page. A reset code will be sent to your registered number.
Q: Can I download my statements?
A: Yes. You can generate them in PDF format under “Statements”.
Q: How do I block my card online?
A: Go to “Card Management” → “Block Card”. The operation is instant.
Q: How do I update my phone number?
A: Visit your branch or contact customer support for verification.
